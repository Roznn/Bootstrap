\frametitle{Confidence interval for a biased estimate $\hat{\theta}$}

 Based on the normal assumption 
$$
\theta \in \lbrack \hat{\theta}-\mathrm{Bias}(\hat{\theta})-z^{(1-\alpha)} \mathrm{se}; \hat{\theta}-\mathrm{Bias}(\hat{\theta})+z^{(1-\alpha)} \mathrm{se}  \rbrack 
$$
}
